Let \(\vvec\) and \(\wvec\) be two vectors in \(\real^n\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation*} \vvec\cdot\wvec = \left[ \begin{array}{c} v_1 \\ v_2 \\ \vdots \\ v_n \\ \end{array} \right] \cdot \left[ \begin{array}{c} w_1 \\ w_2 \\ \vdots \\ w_n \\ \end{array} \right] = v_1w_1 + v_2w_2 + \cdots + v_n w_n\text{.} \end{equation*}
