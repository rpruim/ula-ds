Python practices.

Here are some practices that you may find helpful when working with matrices in Python.
  • Break the matrix entries across lines, one for each row, for better readability by pressing Enter between rows.
    A = np.arry([[ 1, 2, -1, 0],
             [-3, 0,  4, 3 ])
  • For small matrices, you can print your original matrix to check that you have entered it correctly. For larger matrices, you should at least confirm that that shape matches your expectation. You may want to also print labels or to include a dividing line to separate different parts of your output.
    A = np.array([ 1, 2, 2, 2])
print (A)
print ("---------")
print("shape of A =", A.shape)
