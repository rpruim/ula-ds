  1. What is the variance of each of the columns of \(X\text{?}\)
  2. What is the variance of \(2 \xvec_1\text{?}\)
  3. What is the variance of \(2 \xvec_1 + \xvec_3\text{?}\)
  4. What is the variance of \(2 \xvec_1 - \xvec_3\text{?}\)
