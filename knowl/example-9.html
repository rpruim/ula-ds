Example 1.5.4.

NumPy supports several other aggregating functions as well. np.prod(), np.mean(), np.std(), np.var(), np.min(), and np.max() compute the product, mean, standard deviation variance, minimum, and maximum, collapsing over the specified axis or axes. And for boolean arrays, we can use np.all() and np.any().
