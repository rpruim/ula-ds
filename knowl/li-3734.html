Item.

We normalize the vectors \(\xvec_k\) by multiplying by \(\frac{1}{m_k}\text{,}\) where \(m_k\) is the component having the largest absolute value. This is known as the \(L^\infty\) norm of the vector \(\xvec\text{,}\) and we can compute it with np.linalg.norm(x, np.inf) In this way, the vectors \(\overline{\xvec}_k\) approach an eigenvector associated to \(\lambda_1\text{,}\) and the multipliers \(m_k\) approach the eigenvalue \(\lambda_1\text{.}\)
