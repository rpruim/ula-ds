  1. The entries of \(A^{\transpose}A\) are the dot products \(\avec_i\cdot\avec_j\text{.}\)
  2. The entries of \(R^{\transpose}R\) are the dot products \(\rvec_i\cdot\rvec_j\text{.}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle A^{\transpose}A = (QR)^{\transpose}(QR) = R^{\transpose}Q^{\transpose}QR = R^{\transpose}IR = R^{\transpose}R\)
