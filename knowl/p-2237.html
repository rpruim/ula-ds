Students in a school are sometimes absent due to an illness. Suppose that
  • 95% of the students who attend school will attend school the next day.
  • 50% of the students are absent one day will be absent the next day.
We will record the number of present students \(p\) and the number of absent students \(a\) in a state vector \(\xvec=\twovec{p}{a}\) and note that that state vector evolves from one day to the next according to the transition function \(T:\real^2\to\real^2\text{.}\)
