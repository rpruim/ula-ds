Answer 6.5.8.12.1.

  1. \(\yvec\cdot\onevec\) simply sums the components of \(\yvec\text{.}\)
  2. \(\onevec\) is a column of \(A\text{.}\)
  3. \(\yperp\) is in \(\col(A)^\perp\text{.}\)
  4. Use the fact that \(\yhat\) and \(\yperp\) are orthogonal.
  5. This follows from \(\yvec = \yhat + \yperp\text{.}\)
  6. This follows from \(\var(\yvec) = \var(\yhat) + \var(\yperp)\text{.}\)
