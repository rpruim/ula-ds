\begin{align*} \xtilde^{\transpose} \ytilde \amp = (Q \xvec)^{\transpose} (Q \yvec)\\ \amp = \xvec^{\transpose} Q Q \yvec\\ \amp = (\xvec^{\transpose} Q) \yvec \\ \amp = \xvec^{\transpose} \ytilde = \xvec \cdot \ytilde \end{align*}
\(\xtilde \cdot \ytilde = \xtilde \cdot \yvec\) follows by the symmetry of the dot product.
