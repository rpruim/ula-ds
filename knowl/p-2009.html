If \(T : \real^m \to \real^n\) is a linear transformation, then \(T\) is a matrix transformation with matrix
\begin{equation*} A = \begin{bmatrix} T(\evec_1) \amp T(\evec_2) \amp \cdots \amp T(\evec_n) \end{bmatrix}\text{.} \end{equation*}
That is, the columns of the matrix are determined by applying \(T\) to the elementary vectors \(\evec_i\text{.}\)
