For any vectors \(\xvec, \yvec, \zvec \in \real^n\) and scalar \(k \in \real\text{,}\) the following properties hold.
  1. Commutativity.
    \(\displaystyle \xvec \cdot \yvec = \yvec \cdot \zvec\)
  2. Scalar multiples.
    \(\displaystyle k (\xvec \cdot \yvec) = (k \xvec) \cdot \yvec = \xvec \cdot (k \yvec)\)
  3. Length.
    \(\displaystyle \xvec \cdot \xvec = \len{\xvec}^2\)
  4. Distributivity.
    \(\displaystyle \xvec \cdot(\yvec + \zvec) = (\xvec \cdot \yvec) + (\xvec \cdot \zvec)\)
